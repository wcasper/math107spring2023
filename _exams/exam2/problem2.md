## Problem 2

Create a function called *RiemannZeta(s,n)* which takes in two numbers $$s$$ and $$n$$ and returns

$$\frac{1}{1^s} + \frac{1}{2^s} + \frac{1}{3^s} + \frac{1}{4^s} + \dots + \frac{1}{n^s}.$$

Be sure to carefully document your function.
