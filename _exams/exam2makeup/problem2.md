## Problem 2

Create a fucntion called *sine_prod(x,n)* which takes in an integer $$n$$ and a real number $$x$$ and returns the product

$$\sin(x)\sin(2x)\sin(3x)\dots\sin(nx)$$

Be sure to carefully document your function.
